Menu

  • Veggie Combo

    A vegan dish with combination of cabbage and potatoes; beets; gomen (similar to collard greens); lentils, spicy and non spicy; chick peas, spicy and non spicy; and shiro, a stew made of ground chick peas. All are served on “injera” a spongy bread made from the grain “tef”. Price: $12.00

  • Doro Wot

    A classic Ethiopian dish made with chicken in a spicy sauce, served with injera.Flavor-packed Ethiopian stew with tender cooked chicken in a fiery berbere spiced sauce topped with boiled eggs.Traditionally slow-cooked for hours, this instant pot recipe delivers authentic taste in a fraction of the time. Price: $13.00

  • Sheklatibs

    Sliced meat served in a clay dish. Sliced beef or lamb, pan-fried in butter, garlic and onion, tibs is one of the most popular dishes among Ethiopians. Price: $12.00

  • Gomenbesiga

    Gomen be siga is a traditional Ethiopian dish made with fresh ingredients, clarified spiced butter, and lots of onions and garlic for flavor. It consists of sautéed collard greens (gomen) and chunks of beef (siga) as the key ingredients. Price: $10.00

About Lalibela

Here at Lalibela we believe food embodies family, community, tradition, culture, and identity. We cook every dish to share our stories, culture, and traditions with you. We craft every cuisine with love and creativity to make you fall in love not just with Ethiopian food, but also Ethiopian culture. Your meal is skillfully prepared from fresh and authentic ingredients to perfection. Eat what you love, love what you eat, and share it with your loved ones. Enebla! Let’s eat!
